Hello family and friends. Welcome to the start of the **My Family Social** video series. 

I'm Greg Hughlett, the **founder** of My Family Social. 

Most likely if you're watching this video, you're visiting the site for the first time you're wondering, **what is this site all about?**.

My Family Social is all about what families do in this **virtual** world we live in. 

Some people are lucky enough to be close to where all of their family and friends live and can get together regularly in person **and don't need to be virtual**. 

If this is you then you're **very lucky**. Perhaps this site may not make a lot of sense to you.

In my family however, we are spread out everywhere across the country. 

- We get together on some major holidays and family events. 
- However, we do more things virtually than in-person.

Sharing pictures and announcements and other things on social media sites these days, like **Fakebook**, **TikTok and Instagram** isn't always that personal or private. Have you been hacked or impersontated on any of those sites?

My Family Social is dedicated to the notion that what families members and family friends do online should be at the **family level** and should be **private and secure**. 

In My Family Social
- The content posted in your family is **only visible** to members of your family.
- The content in your family cannot be seen or shared with other families on My Family Social.

So What is this content I speak of?

### Show Finder 

- The **Show Finder** let's you quickly find a TV show by entering anything about the tv show, be it **part of the show name**, the **streaming channel**, or even the **family member** who posted the show review. 

- As you can see, it's really fast and accurate and avoids having to scroll through the shows.

- The **Start** and **End dates** filter defaults to a three month range but you could go back earlier if you wish.

- You can enter **comments** on any of the shows (**as you see Julio did**) but comments are not collaborative, meaning you cannot reply to a comment.

- Discussion Threads however are collaborative so members can have a discussion about any subject, be it a TV show, movie, recipes.

### Cricket

The next game to look at is the Cricket dart game.

1. Select **Cricket** ... again I'll select a game of cricket that is currently in progress ... **Cricket-Dripping Dartsters**
2. The game can be played by 2 or 4 players, where 4 players are on two teams. 
3. Each side (or team) takes turns throwing the darts. 
4. The scoreboard indicates which side is to throw. 
5. Notice in Side 2 partner there is a guest.
6. In any of the games you can create one or more guests who are not registered in the family. 
7. In fact, you may be the only family member in the game if you want to use the scoreboard.
8. There are three darts thrown. You enter where each dart hit on the board, click on the submit Turn button.
9. The score is updated automatically updated. **scroll down**
10. Each turn's result is recorded. 
11. There are bonus points calculated but this game has not reached that stage.

### Acquire

1. Select **Acquire** ... select **Cape Cod Acquire Masters**
2. Acquire scorekeeping is done by simply recording the end dollar amounts into the scoreboard. 
3. As you see guests are shown here and we track guests as well, as you see in the **Leaderboard low score**.
